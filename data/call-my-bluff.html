Call My Bluff (BBC One/BBC Two; 1963-2005)

It seems there may have been at least 967 shows and 4649 listed appearances. With 7 people per show, iPlayer and BBC Genome Project and IMDb are missing at least 31% of appearances (and probably a similar percent of people). The host/captains/regulars have included Alan Coren (Team Captain × 610, Panellist × 2), Sandi Toksvig (Team Captain × 550), Bob Holness (Host × 550), Robert Robinson (Host × 331), Frank Muir (Team Captain × 273), Arthur Marshall (Team Captain × 179, Panellist × 2), Patrick Campbell (Team Captain × 93), Fiona Bruce (Host × 60), Rod Liddle (Team Captain × 60), Joanna Lumley (Panellist × 22, Team Captain × 1).
