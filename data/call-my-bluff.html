Call My Bluff (BBC One/BBC Two; 1963-2005)

Key:  male  /  female 

By @astronomyblog & @LoveGraphs. Stats last updated 9 February 2016.

It seems there may have been at least 1044 shows and 5414 listed appearances. With 7 people per show, iPlayer and BBC Genome Project and IMDb are missing at least 26% of appearances (and probably a similar percent of people). The host/captains/regulars have included Alan Coren (Team Captain × 599, Panellist × 6), Bob Holness (Host × 532), Sandi Toksvig (Team Captain × 532), Robert Robinson (Host × 438), Frank Muir (Team Captain × 380), Arthur Marshall (Team Captain × 182, Panellist × 2), Patrick Campbell (Team Captain × 172), Fiona Bruce (Host × 67), Rod Liddle (Team Captain × 67), Joanna Lumley (Panellist × 26, Team Captain × 1).

Notes

Call My Bluff episode data comes from the BBC's Genome Project which has digitised old copies of the Radio Times using OCR. Episodes from 1996 onwards rarely state who was on them so are missing all the guests. For many of those episodes the regulars and host are known even if not listed in the Radio Times.
